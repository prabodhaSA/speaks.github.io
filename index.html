What is SPEAK?


SPEAK is a user-friendly EEG based virtual assistant(mobile application) for Speech Impairments,


Verbal communication is essential to maintain the teacher-student relationship in the normal school learning system. There are cases where children who cannot speak due to accidents or various neurological disorders or genetic conditions, but who can hear, are studying in the normal school education system. In the face of these conditions, there is research on assistive devices based on brain-computer interface technology that can be used for communication, but there is difficulty in using such devices in practice due to the use of expensive equipment and the use of invasive brain-computer interface technology based on surgery for high precision. The primary objective in this study is to determine the ability to use electroencephalography signals with minimal cost and high accuracy for a non-speaking but hearing student to engage in verbal communication in the classroom using SPEAK mobile application.

Background

Verbal communication is essential to maintain the teacher-student relationship in the normal school learning system. There are cases where children who cannot speak due to accidents or various neurological disorders or genetic conditions, but who can hear, are studying in the normal school education system. In the face of these conditions, there is research on assistive devices based on brain-computer interface technology. In our Application generate a speech output to express the direction , answer binary decision question, express the satisfaction and also generate a speech output to express the emotions.

Research Gap

Implement a Voice output for speaking disabled students to communicate in a class room. We used the low-cost MUSE2 headset and analyzed the data using our new framework, which monitors the paradigms of imagined speech and visual imagery in the alpha range

Research Objectives

Implement a Virtual assistant for speaking disabled students to communicate in a class room of age 16-19​.

Create unique Interface to generate left,right, forward, Backward directions​ Design algorithm to reduce overtraining and dimensionality

EEG binary decision brain signal patterns to analyze choice.​ Classification of “Yes / No” choices.​ Responsive “Yes / No” speech sound output.​ Interface reaction feature.

Generate a speech output to express the satisfaction​

Identify emotional signals of speaking impairments using EEG.​ Identify Emotional EEG signals can convert to voice.​ Identify the Emotions​ Spread speak impaired students’ emotional sounds to others

Research Problems


How to generate a speech sound based on direction analysis?​ How to overcome the overlapping signals with other components?


What is producing speech sound of binary decision ?​ What is binary decision ?​ How to generate a speech sound based on the Binary decision analysis?


What is producing speech sound of Satisfaction Rate?​ What is Satisfaction Rate? How to generate a speech sound based on the Satisfaction Rate?


What is producing speech sound of Emotional Analysis?​ What is Emotional Analysis?​ How to generate a speech sound based on the Emotional Analysis?​
